Catch Those Dogs

  • POLISH: 1. randomize spawn location 2. fixed score representation
  • High Concept: Catch as many of the dogs as possible.
  • Player Experience Goals: Want player to keep trying to get a better score.
  • Genre: Arcade
  • Game play: Player uses arrow kays to move around and jump on the screen to collect as many falling dogs as they can
  • Prototype goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating?
  • Features: Spawning an increasing number of dogs every few seconds.
  • Setting/Premise: The game is abstract there is no premise to it.
  • Story: There is no story just the player trying to catch some falling dogs.
  • Target Audience: Those who enjoy trying to beat their best score.
  • Play time: A couple of seconds.
  • Strategies: Just moving around as fast as possible.
  • Prototype Assets: The music and most of the sprites were from other phaser games on the internet.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I can't really think of another I based mie off of.
